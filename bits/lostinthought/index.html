lostinthought

These “bits” I share here are short pieces of information that came up while being in meditative states. While in strictly logical terms they might not make sense to you, for fellow meditators the probably mean alot. Either way, you might still find some of them to be inspiring.

Feel the energy of your heart, and then you will light your being. #lostinthought

At their core, human beings just want to be seen for what they are. So when you decide to drop your assumptions and pay absolute attention, you eyes start glowing and they will pay you back with everything they have: what they are. #lostinthought #spirituality

Within my heart burns the eternal flame (of life). #spirituality #lostinthought

What you defend and extend will ultimately imprison you. But it will work for survival. #lostinthought

Bitterness cannot happen to you if you have a tool to deal with the world. #spirituality #lostinthought

If life pulls you into one direction, don’t pull into the opposite one. Then momentum could happen. #lostinthought

Accept life and you are life. And more than life you cannot be. #lostinthought

When you give everybody what they want then you will be the leader. #lostinthought

Why would you be(!) different from your environment? Put yourself in a healthy environment (at times). #lostinthought

You can only guide people on a path that you have walked before. Otherwise you’re just a leader. #lostinthought

Step aside and let your heart through. #lostinthought

See yourself, see others. #lostinthought

Once you you find the strength to gain a victory over your own demons, for that moment you’ll be able to connect with others deeply. This is because you are recognizing that which people are hiding from themselves. #spirituality #lostinthought

The fear of connection is the fear of disconnection. #lostinthought

We are afraid of ourselfes, and that is what life is. #lostinthought

Hurt me, and you display your delusion. #lostinthought

The beauty of life is that we have it in our hands. #lostinthought

What inspires us is within is. #lostinthought

I chose to not wonder about the things that are not a matter of wondering in the moment. This leaves me sometimes wondering, what I should be wondering about, since this could change every time. This uncertainty is exactly the price I pay to be in the moment. #lostinthought

Not what to do but how to be is a big big question, and everybody is answering it constantly. It’s the question that cannot be dodged. #lostinthought

Show me how scary you are and lose the fight. #lostinthought

When we don’t look at ourselves, we see everything. #lostinthought

Only if you actually believe that you can do all of these items on your daily list, will you give each individual item the care it deserves with the joy that is worth living. #lostinthought

Being surrounded by very capable people, who could very well bring you down if they wanted to, can seem threatening. However this really is the kind of situation we need to be longing for, because we would be blessed if everybody reached his full potential. #lostinthought

Don’t get confused by your own looks, as they don’t define you, unless you get confused by your own looks. #lostinthought

With a substantial amount of ignorance and a substantial amount of arrogance you could dominate anybody. Even kill a person without regret. That’s in part why people are not spiritual. It would mean taking down the weapons #lostinthought

Love is life (in possibly every imaginable way. work, relationships, values etc.) #lostinthought

What do we know about who we want to be? #lostinthought

The rules of causality, or in other words, the fact that one action (the “reaction”) must be in a relationship to a previous action, are rules the heart does not (have to) follow. Listening to / being with the heart is thereby hugely relaxing. #lostinthought

Explaining something, makes something less beautiful and also risks missing the point entirely #lostinthought

In any case, we look at the beauty within #lostinthought

We need to stop rewarding looks, emotional and mental juggles, because it is beings that connect and thereby we need to reward great beings #lostinthought

No need to judge, no need to look, when the gut already know what’s good. #lostinthought #rhiming

Don’t get used to the emotions you feel and the energies you wield… because they need to transform. #lostinthought

From our first moment to our last moment we are free to _________ (fill in the blank). #lostinthought

Attachment is salvation for the mono-focused / focus-limited. #lostinthought

The way we usually use our focus is not very spiritual #lostinthought

Different forms of life are always available. #lostinthought

Your flying altitude is the depth you risk falling into. #lostinthought

If you’re running around with a guitar and let everbody you meet play a note, there’s never going to come a nice song out of that. #lostinthought

Once you lose a map, the notes you made about the shortcuts lose their meaning #lostinthought

There is no alternative to just being oneself. There is a place where the heart knows this so clearly, that the mind is not able to create any illusions about it. #lostinthought

Truely giving up on happyness and easing into whatever is there on all levels turns suffering into pain and being into joy. #lostinthought #enlightenment

As long as you feel bad there is always this urge to solve problems, to get busy and to chase this and that goal with no end in sight. As soon as you feel good, well, there isn’t. #meditation #calmingdown #lostinthought

Meeting a passionate woman is always a dance, a playful back and forth, and never an act, or even a piece of work. #lostinthought

Once you face something, not in anger but in awe, something new can happen. #lostinthought

It wouldn’t make sense to speak in a non-learning environment. #lostinthought

Start listening to your state of being and you can stop watching the movie of your projection #meditation #lostinthought

Being emotionally and physically unrelaxed / tense is usefull to override and control ones own undesired natural reactions. Being uncomfortable is not an accident, it is a tool we chose to use. #lostinthought

The key to start maintaining relaxation is to get relaxed about moments of stress. This enables you to start the relaxation process again, while getting better and better at observing your own stress. #lostinthought

Emotional and physical comfort is where mental stability is easily maintained. This way comfort is nothing to look down upon #lostinthought

The more of yourself you are able to recognize in the other, the stronger the connection. #lostinthought

Pain becomes just another state of being once I decide to make peace with it. Pain is not suffering, only I can create that. #lostinthought
